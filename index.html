Our External Faculties

Babeesh Aneela


Babeesh Anela is an Indian artist from Kerala who is the chief artist of Bhaavageetham Art Gallery, Trivandrum. He was a first class diploma holder in 2002-2004 batch of School of Fine Arts. He holds a national diploma in Kerala Mural painting from Guruvayoor Devasom Institute of Mural Painting in 2010.His major works include the painting done at:
Chowallor Shiva Temple, Guruvayoor
Ganapathyvattom Ganapathy Temple, Sulthan Bathery Wayanad

Kalamandalam Bindhulekha


Kalamandalam Bindhulekha is a talented, self-assured young woman fast gaining acclaim for her work in a traditional male bastion – the temple art of mural painting in Kerala. Belying her matter-of-fact manner, she expresses herself through the use of vivid hues in her paintings. Elemental reds, blues and yellows reveal the rich landscape of her mind.She follows her dreams, creating her own opportunities and powering through life with self-confidence.

Sreeraj VS


Sreeraj is an independent professional artist . He is a highly imaginative person with an inherent talent and passion for variety of Arts. He obtained his bachelor in Fine Arts from R.L.V Music and fine art college in 2011. With the use of bright colors coupled with the wonderful freedom of his brushstrokes and spontaneity in forms he creates works that are truly magical, inspiring, spiritual and alluring. Art work he creates is like a visual treat with minutest of detailing of the clothes, jewelry etc. . His works inspired from Indian murals and Indian culture
